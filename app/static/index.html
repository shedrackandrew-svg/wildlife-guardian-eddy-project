About WildGuard

WildGuard is a wildlife intelligence and conservation platform dedicated to practical habitat awareness, field support, and species-centered monitoring workflows. Our mission is to empower conservation teams, researchers, and field biologists with real-time intelligence and AI-driven insights.

Designed for professional clarity and rapid field interpretation, WildGuard combines cutting-edge AI detection with comprehensive species knowledge, habitat mapping, and detection analytics—all accessible from one unified platform.
